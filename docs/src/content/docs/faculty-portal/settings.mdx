## Changing Your Semester View

Some pages show data for a specific semester. To switch:

1. Look for the **Semester/School Year Selector** (in the sidebar or page header).
2. Choose the semester (1st or 2nd) and school year (e.g., 2025-2026).
3. The page updates to show data for that period.

This affects what you see on the Grades, Attendance, and other academic pages.

---

## Switching Active School

If your institution has multiple campuses or schools, you can switch between them using the **Active School** selector. This changes which school's data you are viewing across the portal.
