The **Stream** tab on your class detail page is where you communicate with your students and manage coursework.

---

## Creating a Post

1. Open the class detail page.
2. Go to the **Stream** tab.
3. Click **Create Post** (or use the quick action **Post Announcement**).
4. Fill in the form:

| Field | What It Is |
|---|---|
| **Title** | A clear title for your post |
| **Content** | The body of your message or instructions |
| **Type** | Announcement, Assignment, Quiz, or Activity |
| **Status** | Backlog, In Progress, In Review, Done, Blocked |
| **Priority** | Low, Medium, High |
| **Start Date** | When the post becomes visible to students |
| **Due Date** | When it's due (for assignments, quizzes, activities) |
| **Progress** | 0–100% completion (auto-set to 100 when marked Done) |
| **Total Points** | Maximum points for graded work |

5. For **Assignments**, you can also set:
   - **Instructions** — Detailed submission guidelines
   - **Audience** — All students or specific students only
   - **Rubric** — Grading criteria with performance levels

6. Attach files or links if needed.
7. Click **Create**.

The post appears immediately in the class stream for students to see.

---

## Editing or Deleting a Post

- Click **Edit** on any post to modify its content, dates, or settings.
- Click **Delete** to remove the post. Any attached files are also deleted.

---

## Changing a Post's Status

Click the status label on a post to cycle through: **Backlog → In Progress → In Review → Done → Blocked**.

This helps you track which assignments you are actively working on. When you mark a post as **Done**, its progress is automatically set to 100%.

---

## Viewing Student Submissions

For assignments, quizzes, and activities where students submit work:

1. Click on the post in the stream.
2. Click **View Submissions**.
3. You will see each student's submission with:
   - Student name and ID
   - The content they submitted (text or files)
   - Submission date and time
   - Submission status (submitted, graded)
   - Points awarded

---

## Grading a Submission

1. Open the submissions list for a post.
2. Find the student.
3. Enter their **Points** (0 to the total points you set).
4. Click **Grade**.

The student sees their grade immediately on their class detail page.
