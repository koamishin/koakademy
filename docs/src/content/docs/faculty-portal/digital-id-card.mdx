---
title: Digital ID Card
description: View and use your faculty digital ID card with QR code verification.
---

Click **ID Card** in the navigation menu to see your faculty digital ID.

---

## What Your ID Card Shows

- Your school's logo and name
- Your photo
- Your full name
- Your faculty ID number
- Your department
- A **QR code** that can be scanned to verify your identity

---

## Verifying Your ID

Anyone can scan the QR code on your ID card using any QR scanner app (iPhone Camera, Android Camera, or a dedicated QR app). The scan opens a verification page showing:
- Whether you are currently an active faculty member
- Your name and department
- When the card was issued

No login is required to verify — the page is public.

---

## Refreshing Your QR Code

Click **Refresh** to generate a new QR code. Use this to get a fresh verification token for the current session.
