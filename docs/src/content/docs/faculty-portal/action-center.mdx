Click **Action Center** in the navigation menu to see all your pending work across all classes.

---

## What You See

The Action Center shows:

- **Stats bar** at the top:
  - Active assignments count
  - Completed count
  - High-priority (focus) items
  - Overall completion percentage
  - Status breakdown by category

- **Activity cards** for every assignment, quiz, and activity you've posted across all your classes

Each activity card shows:
- Title and type (Assignment, Quiz, Activity)
- Which class it belongs to — subject code and section
- Due date range
- Current status and priority level
- Progress bar (0–100%)
- Who it's assigned to

---

## Managing Activities

- Click the **status label** on any card to change it (Backlog, In Progress, Review, Done, Blocked)
- Click the **card** itself to go directly to that class's stream page

The Action Center is your productivity hub — a single place to see everything that needs your attention without opening each class individually.
