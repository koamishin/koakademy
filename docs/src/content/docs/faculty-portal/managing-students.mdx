The **Students** tab on your class detail page is where you manage who is enrolled in your class.

---

## Adding an Existing Student

1. Open the class detail page.
2. Go to the **Students** tab.
3. Click **Add Student**.
4. Search for a student by name, student ID, or LRN. Type at least 2 characters to see results.
5. The search results show:
   - **In This Class** — Already enrolled here (can't add again)
   - **In Other Section** — Enrolled in another section of the same subject
   - **Available** — Can be added to your class
6. Click on the student to add them. They are enrolled immediately.

---

## Creating a New SHS Student (On the Spot)

For Senior High School classes, you can create a brand-new student record directly from your class:

1. Open the SHS class detail page.
2. Go to the **Students** tab.
3. Click **Create SHS Student**.
4. Fill in the required information:
   - **LRN** (Learner Reference Number, 12 digits)
   - **Last Name**, **First Name**, **Middle Name**
   - **Birth Date** and **Gender**
   - **Contact** number
   - **Strand** and **Grade Level** (11 or 12)
5. Check **Enroll in This Class** to add them immediately.
6. Click **Create**.

The student is created in the system and enrolled in your class in one step.

---

## Removing a Student

1. Open the class detail page.
2. Go to the **Students** tab.
3. Find the student you want to remove.
4. Click the **Remove** (trash) icon next to their name.
5. Confirm the removal.

The student's enrollment is soft-deleted. They can be re-added later if needed.

---

## Transferring a Student to Another Section

If a student needs to move to a different section of the same subject:

1. Find the student in the roster.
2. Click **Request Move**.
3. Select the **Target Class** from the dropdown — shows other sections of the same subject with available slots.
4. Click **Send Request**.

The other section's teacher receives a notification. When they accept, the student is automatically moved to their class.

### Accepting an Incoming Transfer

If another teacher sends a student to your section:

1. You will receive a notification.
2. Click the notification or go to your class detail page.
3. The student will be added to your roster when you accept the transfer.

---

## Exporting Your Student List

1. Open the class detail page.
2. Click **Export Student List**.
3. Choose your format:
   - **CSV** — Download immediately as a spreadsheet
   - **PDF** — A formatted PDF will be generated in the background. You will receive a notification when it's ready.
