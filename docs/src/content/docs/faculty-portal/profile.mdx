---
title: Profile
description: Update your personal information, faculty details, and profile picture in the faculty portal.
---

Click **Profile** in the navigation menu to manage your personal information.

---

## Personal Information

You can update:
- Your name
- Your email address
- Your phone number
- Your address (street, city, state, country, postal code)
- A short bio about yourself
- Your department and position
- Your website (if you have one)
- Your profile picture (upload a new photo)

Click **Save** after making changes.

---

## Faculty Information

The faculty info section shows details from your employment record:
- Your faculty ID number
- Your department
- Your office hours

If your school allows it, you may be able to edit some of these fields. Click **Update Faculty Info** after making changes.
