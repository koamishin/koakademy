## Global Search for Classes

Use the search bar in the faculty portal to quickly find your classes. Type a subject code, section name, or school year. Results show matching classes with their subject code, title, section, and school year.

---

## Global Search for Students

You can also search for students across all your classes. Type a student's name or student ID to find them.

Results show:
- Student name, ID, course, and year level
- How many of your classes they are in
- Which sections they belong to

Click a result to view the student's full information — name, course, contact details, and photo.
