title: Help & Support
description: Submit help tickets for problems, issues, or suggestions, and track responses from school staff.
---

If you have a problem, question, or suggestion, submit a help ticket.

---

## Viewing Your Tickets

1. Click **Help** in the navigation menu.
2. You will see a list of all your submitted tickets, showing their status (open, in progress, resolved) and priority.

---

## Creating a New Ticket

1. Click **Create Ticket**.
2. Choose the **Type**:
   - **Support** — You need help with something
   - **Issue** — Something is broken or not working
   - **Recommendation** — You have a suggestion
3. Write a clear **Subject** (e.g., "Cannot record attendance for CC101").
4. Write a detailed **Message** explaining the problem.
5. Choose the **Priority**:
   - **Low** — Not urgent
   - **Medium** — Needs attention soon
   - **High** — Urgent, blocking your work
6. Optionally attach files (screenshots, documents, max 10 MB each).
7. Click **Submit**.

---

## Replying to a Ticket

1. Open the ticket from your list.
2. Read the conversation thread.
3. Type your reply in the message box.
4. Optionally attach files.
5. Click **Reply**.

School staff will see your reply and respond. You will receive a notification when they do.
