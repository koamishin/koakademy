## Grades Overview (All Classes)

Click **Grades** in the navigation menu to see grading status across all your classes.

Each class appears as a card showing:
- Subject code, title, and section
- Number of students
- Room
- Schedule summary
- A link to **Manage Grades** for that class

Use this page to quickly see which classes still need grades entered. Click any class to go directly to its grade sheet.

---

## Viewing the Grade Sheet

1. Open the class detail page.
2. Go to the **Grades** tab.

You will see a table with every student and four grade columns:
- **Prelim** — Preliminary period grade (0–100)
- **Midterm** — Midterm period grade (0–100)
- **Finals** — Final period grade (0–100)
- **Average** — The overall average

---

## Entering Grades

1. In the **Grades** tab, click on a student's grade cell.
2. Type the numeric grade (0–100, you can use decimals).
3. Press Enter or click outside the cell to move to the next.
4. Click **Save Grades** when you are done to save all changes at once.

<Aside type="tip">
Use the Tab key to move quickly between cells when entering grades for multiple students.
</Aside>

---

## Auto-Average Setting

Depending on your school's settings, the **Average** column may be:
- **Auto-calculated** — The system computes it from Prelim, Midterm, and Finals
- **Manual** — You enter it yourself

Check the toggle or indicator on the grade sheet to see which mode is active.

---

## Submitting Grades

When you have finished entering grades for a grading period:

1. Click **Submit Grades**.
2. Select which term you are submitting (Prelim, Midterm, or Finals).
3. Confirm the submission.

<Aside type="caution">
Once grades are submitted and finalized by the registrar, you may not be able to edit them without special permission. Double-check your entries before submitting.
</Aside>
