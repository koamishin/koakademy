---
title: Notifications
description: View and manage your faculty notifications — student transfers, export completions, and school announcements.
---

Click the **bell icon** in the navigation bar to see your notifications. A number on the bell shows how many unread notifications you have.

---

## What You'll Be Notified About

- Student transfer requests from other faculty
- PDF exports that are ready to download (attendance reports, student lists)
- Assessment and report generation completions
- School announcements

---

## Managing Notifications

- Click a notification to **mark it as read**.
- Click **Mark All as Read** to clear everything at once.
- Click the delete icon on any notification to **remove it**.
