---
title: Announcements
description: View school-wide announcements including faculty meetings, deadlines, policy updates, and events.
---

Click **Announcements** in the navigation menu to see all school announcements, including:
- Enrollment schedules
- Faculty meetings and deadlines
- Grading submission reminders
- System maintenance notices
- School events

Each announcement shows:
- A title
- The full message
- When it was posted
- The announcement type (Information, Warning, Urgent, etc.)
