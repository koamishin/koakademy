The **Dashboard** is the first page you see after signing in. It gives you a complete overview of your teaching day and semester.

---

## How to Sign In

1. Open your web browser and go to your school's Koakademy website.
2. Enter your **faculty email address** and **password**.
3. Click **Sign In**.

If this is your first time signing in, you may be asked to verify your identity by entering your **Faculty ID Number**. This is a one-time verification.

---

## What You See on the Dashboard

### Stats Cards

At the top, four cards show:
- **Active Classes** — How many classes you are teaching this semester
- **Total Students** — Combined enrollment across all your classes
- **Total Hours** — Your teaching load in hours per week
- **Performance** — Your overall performance score

### Upcoming Classes

Shows your next 5 classes with subject, section, room, and number of students. This helps you prepare for what's coming up.

### Today's Schedule

Shows only the classes happening today, with their times and rooms. This is your "what do I have right now" view.

### Weekly Schedule

A Monday through Saturday grid showing all your classes. Each entry shows the subject, section, room, and time slot.

### Recent Activity

A feed of recent actions from your classes:
- "Student [Name] enrolled in [Class]"
- "Post [Title] was created"
- "Attendance session for [Date] was recorded"
- "Grades were updated in [Class]"

### Attendance Chart

A chart showing attendance trends over the last 3 months. You can see present, late, absent, and excused counts for your classes and filter by specific classes.

### Calendar

Upcoming school events for the next 2 months, color-coded by event type.

### Your Digital ID Card

A compact card with your photo, name, faculty ID, and a QR code. Anyone can scan the code to verify your identity. Click **View Full** to see the full-sized card, or **Refresh** to update the QR code.

### Announcements

School-wide announcements relevant to faculty, including meeting schedules, deadlines, and policy updates.
