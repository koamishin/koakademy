## Changing Your Password

1. Go to **Profile → Security** (or **Profile → Password**).
2. Enter your **current password**.
3. Enter your **new password** twice for confirmation.
4. Click **Change Password**.

<Aside type="tip">
Choose a password that is at least 8 characters long and includes letters, numbers, and symbols.
</Aside>

## Setting Up Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security. After entering your password, you also need a code from an authenticator app on your phone.

**How to set it up:**
1. Go to **Profile → Security**.
2. Click **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**.
3. A QR code appears on screen.
4. Open your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.), tap Add Account, and scan the QR code.
5. Enter the 6-digit code from the app to confirm.
6. **Save your recovery codes.** These 8 codes let you sign in if you lose your phone. Write them down or store them safely.

**How to disable 2FA:**
1. Go to **Profile → Security**.
2. Click **Disable Two-Factor Authentication**.
3. Enter your password to confirm.

## Login Challenge Toggle

Choose whether the system asks for your 2FA code every time you log in. Toggle **Require Two-Factor at Login** on or off.

---

## Email Authentication

If enabled, the system sends a verification code to your email when you sign in from a new device. Toggle **Email Authentication** on or off.

---

## Recovery Codes

Recovery codes let you sign in if you lose your phone or cannot use your authenticator app. Each code works only once.

- To get new codes, click **Regenerate Recovery Codes**. This invalidates any previous codes.
- Store your recovery codes in a safe place.

## Managing Browser Sessions

This section shows all the devices and browsers where you are signed in. Each session shows the device, browser, IP address, and last activity time.

If you see a session you don't recognize, or used a public computer and forgot to log out:

1. Click **Log Out Other Browser Sessions**.
2. Enter your password to confirm.

This signs you out everywhere except the device you are currently using.

## Passkeys (Passwordless Login)

Passkeys let you sign in without a password using your fingerprint, face, or device PIN.

### Setting Up a Passkey
1. Go to **Profile → Security → Passkeys**.
2. Click **Add Passkey**.
3. Your browser prompts you to verify your identity.
4. Give the passkey a name (e.g., "My iPhone" or "Laptop").
5. Click **Save**.

### Signing In with a Passkey
Next time you sign in, choose **Sign in with Passkey** instead of entering your password.

### Removing a Passkey
1. Go to **Profile → Security → Passkeys**.
2. Find the passkey.
3. Click **Delete**.
