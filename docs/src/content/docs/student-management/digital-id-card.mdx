title: Digital ID Card
description: View your student ID card with QR code that anyone can scan to verify your enrollment status.
---

Your digital student ID has a QR code that anyone can scan to verify your identity.

## Viewing your ID card

- **Dashboard widget**: A mini ID card appears on your Dashboard. Click it to open the full version.
- **Full page**: Go to **ID Card** from the sidebar, or click the ID card widget on your Dashboard.

The full ID card shows:
- Your photo
- Full name and student ID
- Course and year level
- School logo
- QR code

## Refreshing the QR code

Click **Refresh QR Code** to generate a new QR code for the current session. This updates the timestamp embedded in the code.

## How others verify your ID

1. Someone scans your QR code with any QR scanner app (most phone cameras can do this).
2. The scanner opens a verification page.
3. The page confirms whether your student status is valid.
4. No login is required for verification — anyone can verify.
