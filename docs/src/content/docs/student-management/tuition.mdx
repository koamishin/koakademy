Go to **Tuition** from the sidebar. This is where you check your payments.

## Balance overview

At the top of the page, you'll see:
- **Total balance** — how much you still owe
- **Total paid** — how much you've paid so far
- **Downpayment** — your initial payment
- **Discount** — any discount applied
- **Payment progress** — a colored bar showing what percentage is paid
- **Status** — "Fully Paid" or "Partially Paid"

## Fee breakdown

Below the overview, you'll see:
- Lecture fees
- Laboratory fees
- Miscellaneous fees
- Overall tuition (the total)

## Transaction history

A table showing every payment you've made:
- Date paid
- Description
- Amount
- Status (Completed / Pending)
- Invoice number
- Payment method

## Filtering by semester

Use the dropdown at the top to switch between semesters and school years. This lets you view tuition records from past semesters.

## Statement of Account

Click **Statement of Account** (or go to `/student/tuition/soa`) for a printable version of your billing statement. This page includes:
- Your student information and course
- Complete fee breakdown
- Payment history
- School letterhead and logo

**How to use it**: Print or save this page as a PDF when you need an official copy of your billing statement.

<Aside type="tip">
Print your Statement of Account before the semester ends for your records.
</Aside>
