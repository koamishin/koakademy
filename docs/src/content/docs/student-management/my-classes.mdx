Go to **My Classes** from the sidebar or navigation menu. This page has two tabs.

## Curriculum Checklist tab

This shows every subject required for your course, organized by year and semester.

Each subject is color-coded:

| Color | Status | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 🟢 Green | **Completed** | You passed this subject |
| 🔵 Blue | **Ongoing** | You're taking it now |
| ⚪ Gray | **Pending** | You haven't taken it yet |
| 🔴 Red | **Failed** | You need to retake it |

A **progress bar** at the top shows what percentage of your total units you've earned.

**How to use it**: Use this to plan which subjects you still need. If a subject shows "Failed," talk to your adviser about retaking it.

## Current Classes tab

This shows all the classes you're enrolled in this semester as cards.

Each card shows:
- Subject code and title
- Section name
- Faculty member's name
- Schedule (days and times)
- Room number

**How to use it**: Click any card to open the full class detail page. Use the **room filter** at the top to find classes in a specific room.

## Class Detail Page

When you click a class, you get a full page with five tabs.

### Posts tab

This is where your teacher posts announcements, assignments, and materials.

- **Announcements**: Read-only messages from your teacher.
- **Assignments**: Shows the due date and your submission status (Not Submitted / Submitted / Graded).

#### How to submit an assignment

1. Click on an assignment post.
2. Write your answer in the text box.
3. Attach files if needed (up to 50 MB each).
4. Click **Submit**.

<Aside type="caution">
You can only submit once per assignment. Double-check your work before clicking Submit.
</Aside>

After submitting, the assignment shows your status and, once graded, your score.

### Schedule tab

Shows the day-by-day schedule for this class — day of the week, start time, end time, room, and any notes from your teacher.

### Grades tab

Shows your grades for this class:
- **Prelim** — First grading period
- **Midterm** — Second grading period
- **Finals** — Final grading period
- **Average** — Your overall grade

### Attendance tab

- **Stats bar** at the top shows how many times you were Present, Late, Absent, or Excused.
- **History list** below shows every session with the date, your status, the topic covered, and any remarks.

### People tab

Shows all your classmates with their names and profile pictures.
