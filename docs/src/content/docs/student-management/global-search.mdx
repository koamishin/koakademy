The **search bar** in the navigation bar lets you find things quickly without navigating through menus.

## How to search

1. Click the search bar, or press the `/` key on your keyboard.
2. Start typing — results appear after you type at least 2 characters.
3. Results are grouped by category:
   - **Subjects** — find subjects by code or title
   - **Classes** — find class sections
   - **Courses** — find academic programs
   - **Enrollments** — your own enrollment records
4. Click any result to go directly to that item.

## Tips

- Search by **subject code** (e.g. "MATH101") to quickly jump to a class.
- Search by **course name** to find information about a program.
- Only **your own** enrollment records appear in results — you won't see other students' data.
