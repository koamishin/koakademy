Go to **Profile** from the sidebar or user menu. This page has multiple tabs for managing your information.

## Personal Info tab

Edit your account details:
- Name, email, phone number
- Address (city, state, country, postal code)
- Bio and website
- Upload a profile photo

Click **Save** after making changes.

## Student Info tab

View and edit your school record:
- Full name, student ID, email, phone
- Address, civil status, nationality, religion
- Birth date, gender
- Emergency contact
- Course and year level

## Contacts sub-section

- Personal contact number
- Emergency contact name, phone number, address, and relationship
- Facebook profile link

## Education sub-section

Your previous schools:
- Elementary school and graduation year
- Junior high school and graduation year
- Senior high school and graduation year

## Parents sub-section

- Father's full name
- Mother's full name

<Aside type="note">
Some fields may be read-only depending on your school's settings. Contact your registrar if you need to update locked information.
</Aside>
