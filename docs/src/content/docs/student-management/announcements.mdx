title: Announcements
description: Read school-wide announcements about enrollment, tuition deadlines, events, class suspensions, and more.
---

Go to **Announcements** from the sidebar to see all school-wide messages.

The page shows a feed of announcements with:
- Title and date posted
- Category (Information, Warning, Critical, Maintenance, etc.)
- Full message content
- Any attached links or action buttons (e.g. "Enroll Now," "View Tuition")

Announcements also appear on your **Dashboard** as a compact feed, so you don't miss important updates.

## Types of announcements you might see

- Enrollment opening dates
- Tuition payment deadlines
- Class suspensions (e.g. due to weather)
- System maintenance schedules
- Upcoming events and activities
