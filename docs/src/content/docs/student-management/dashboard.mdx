After signing in to the student portal, you land on your **Dashboard**. This is your home page that gives you a quick summary of everything important.

## What you see

| Section | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Student info card** | Your student ID, full name, course, and year level |
| **Enrolled classes** | Each class you're taking this semester, with your current grades (Prelim, Midterm, Finals, Average) |
| **Tuition balance** | How much you still owe for this semester |
| **Clearance status** | Whether you're cleared for the current semester |
| **Digital ID card** | A mini version of your student ID with a QR code |
| **Announcements** | Important school-wide messages and reminders |

## What you can do from here

- **Click any class card** to go to that class's detail page.
- **Click your ID card** to open the full-size version.
- **Click announcements** to read the full message.

<Aside type="tip">
Check your Dashboard daily — it shows new grades, payment updates, and school announcements as soon as they're available.
</Aside>
