title: Notifications
description: Manage your in-app notifications — mark them as read, clear all, or delete individual notifications.
---

The **bell icon** 🔔 in the top navigation bar shows your notifications.

- A **red badge** shows how many unread notifications you have.
- Click the bell to open the dropdown.
- Each notification shows a title, message, and how long ago it was sent.

## Managing notifications

- **Click a notification** to mark it as read.
- Click **Mark all as read** to clear the badge and mark everything read at once.
- Click the **delete icon** on any notification to remove it permanently.

## What notifications you'll receive

Notifications include things like:
- Enrollment confirmations
- Grade postings
- Payment receipts
- School announcements
- Assessment PDFs that are ready for download
