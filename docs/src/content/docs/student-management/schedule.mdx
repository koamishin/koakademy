---
title: My Schedule
description: View your weekly timetable — all your classes at a glance with times, rooms, and teachers.
---

Go to **Schedule** from the sidebar. This shows your weekly timetable — all your classes in one view.

Each card shows:
- Subject and section
- Teacher
- Day and time (e.g. "Mon/Wed 10:00 AM – 11:30 AM")
- Room number

**How to use it**: Check this page to see where you need to be and when. Use the **room filter** at the top if you want to find all your classes in a specific building.
